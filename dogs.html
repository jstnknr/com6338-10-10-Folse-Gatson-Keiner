Interesting Facts About Dogs

Dogs are amazing creatures. Here are some interesting facts:

  • Dogs have an incredible sense of smell, far superior to humans.
  • The Basenji dog breed doesn't bark, but they can yodel.
  • Dogs are capable of understanding up to 250 words and gestures.
  • The Newfoundland breed has webbed feet and is an excellent swimmer.
